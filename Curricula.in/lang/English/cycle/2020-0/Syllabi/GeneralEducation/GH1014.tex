\begin{justification}
The objective of the course is for the student to understand the interrelation that exists between political and economic systems of a country or region. The thread of this course will be the book Why Nations Fail: The Origins of Power, Prosperity, and Povert by Acemoglu-Robinson.
At the end of the course, students must have learned an informed interpretation of different social dynamics through which power is organized and distributed, be it of a symbolic, economic and/or political nature. This course aims to train the students ability to use more complex concepts and to develop more elaborate interpretations of reality. 
\end{justification}
