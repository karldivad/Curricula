\begin{justification}
It offers a wide vision of the ethical and professional aspects related to computing. The topics included cover ethical, social and political aspects. The moral dimensions of computing. The methods and tools of analysis. Administration of computer resources. Security and control of computer systems. Professional and ethical responsibilities. Intellectual property.
\end{justification}

\begin{goals}
\item Make the student understand the importance of care and ethics in the transfer and use of information.
\item To instill in the student that the trends of technological improvement should not lead to the degradation of the morals of society.
\end{goals}
